About Us

our history

YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD has been importing New and Used vehicles from Japan and other countries for over 15 years. Providing exceptional quality products and farming lifetime relationships with clients are the cornerstones for YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD's success. YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD has catered to a variety of clients from all over the island and maintained the relationships built from day one. Reliability in the automobile industry of Sri Lanka is of heavy importance to all individuals, thus YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD stands by their product and alongside their client. The team at YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD are always learning and striving to become the best at what they do, to maintain YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD's mark in the industry and grow further while taking on the challenges the future brings. We invite you to become a part of our story.

We import all type of Japanese cars including major manufacturer such as Toyota, Honda, Nissan,Suzuki, Mitsubishi, Mazda, Lexus, Subaru and all new arrivals

The company Director Operations at YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD vision is to be the leading used vehicle importer among the Sri Lanka as well as all over the world.

Our aims to provide our customers to the best of our ability and provide prompt, accurate and efficient service to our valuable customers ensure that they are satisfied with our service and the product that we imported for them.

our Vision

to be the leading customer-focused company in Sri Lanka's automotive industry, dedicated to helping our clients achieve their goals and dreams with exceptional service and support.

our mission

At YAMAGIN CAR IMPORTERS PVT LTD, we see ourselves as partners with our clients, employees, and the community. Our mission is to maintain our strong brand reputation by delivering the most dependable vehicles in the industry. We are committed to sustainability, trust, and consistent growth, ensuring long-term success for our customers and business alike.
