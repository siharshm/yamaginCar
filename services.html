Our Services

Vehicle registration

Once your imported vehicle arrives in Sri Lanka, it must be registered with the Department of Motor Traffic (DMT) to ensure legal compliance and roadworthiness. Our team handles the entire registration process, including document preparation, vehicle inspections, revenue license applications, and obtaining the official registration certificate. We streamline the process, saving you time and ensuring a hassle-free experience, so you can get your vehicle on the road without delays.

Free first three service

To ensure your imported vehicle remains in optimal condition, we offer three complimentary services after your purchase. Our expert technicians will conduct thorough inspections, oil changes, and essential maintenance checks to enhance performance and longevity. This exclusive service package guarantees a smooth driving experience while giving you peace of mind about your vehicle’s reliability.

Original Japan warranty

All our imported vehicles come with the Original Japan Warranty, ensuring authenticity and reliability. This warranty covers manufacturer defects and certain mechanical components, giving you confidence in the quality of your purchase. With genuine warranty support, you can enjoy peace of mind, knowing your vehicle is protected by global standards.

After sales maintaining free advice

We believe in providing long-term support beyond just selling vehicles. Our experts offer free after-sales maintenance advice to help you keep your imported vehicle in top condition. Whether it’s routine servicing, troubleshooting minor issues, or best practices for longevity, we are here to guide you with professional recommendations—ensuring a smooth and hassle-free ownership experience.

leasing facilities

We offer flexible leasing facilities to make owning your dream vehicle easier and more affordable. Partnering with leading financial institutions, we provide tailored leasing options with competitive interest rates and convenient repayment plans. Our team assists you throughout the process, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience so you can drive away with confidence.

Clearing and forwarding

We provide professional clearing and forwarding services to ensure a smooth and hassle-free import process for your vehicle. Our team handles all customs clearance procedures, tax documentation, and port-related formalities efficiently, ensuring timely delivery of your vehicle. With our expertise, you can avoid unnecessary delays and additional costs, making the import process seamless and stress-free.
